Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Jun Zhao. My research interests broadly include Natural Language Processing, Information Extraction and Large Language Models. I have published several papers in top-tier conferences such as AAAI, ACL, EMNLP, CIKM and so on.
