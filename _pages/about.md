I am a 4th-year PhD student in the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I am supervised by Prof. [Jun Zhao](http://nlpr-web.ia.ac.cn/cip/english/~junzhao/index.html). My research interests lie in Event Extraction and Event Relation Extraction.
